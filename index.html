My Projects

Captain Cosmic's Rocketship Journey

Unity C# OpenAI API

As part of a collaborative project with NSC Creative, my team developed a 3D space adventure game aimed at educating children aged 7–12 about the Outer Planets. The game featured an AI-powered avatar that shared fun, educational facts through interactive gameplay.

Alongside development, we created a business deck covering monetisation, marketing, and target audience. This was presented to the client as part of our final delivery.

In the end, the project successfully blended education with entertainment and commercial strategy. NSC Creative praised our stragetic thinking and professional presentation.

Key Features:

  • Fun and interactive gameplay mechanics
  • Integration with OpenAI API to create a unique and educational avatar
  • Well-designed UI elements that resonates with our target audience
  • Strong business strategy and commercial planning

Energy City Run

Unity C#

An educational endless runner game teaching players about sustainable energy sources and environmental conservation. Players navigate through a city collecting renewable energy tokens while avoiding pollution obstacles.

Key Features:

  • Procedurally generated city levels for endless gameplay
  • Educational facts about renewable energy integrated into gameplay
  • Dynamic difficulty scaling based on player performance
  • Energy-themed power-ups and special abilities

Technical Highlights:

Developed an optimized procedural generation system that creates diverse urban environments while maintaining consistent performance across various device capabilities. Implemented a custom physics system for smooth character movement and obstacle interaction.
